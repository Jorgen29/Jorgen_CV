About me

I am a motivated and detail-oriented Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) graduate, with a strong foundation in Computer System Servicing (CSS) gained during my Senior High School studies. I have a deep passion for using technology to solve real-world problems, particularly in the areas of web development, database management, and technical support.

During my academic journey, I completed my OJT at PRECOCE as a web developer, where I designed databases and developed both front-end and back-end functionalities, systems and websites for client projects. I also served as an IT technician at PCKEN, where I performed computer repairs, software installations, and routine maintenance tasks. These experiences sharpened both my development and troubleshooting skills.

In addition to my academic and hands-on experience, I spent five years as an assistant manager at a Cignal TV dealership, overseeing operations, customer service, and technical installations. I also manage my own small computer shop, where I assist clients with various IT needs and business operations. These roles have helped me become adaptable, resourceful, and customer-oriented.

I am committed to continuous learning and growth. Whether it's mastering new development frameworks, enhancing database systems, or delivering excellent support, I am always eager to contribute meaningfully to the organizations I work with.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring emerging technologies, upgrading computer systems, and sharing my knowledge with others.
